Swimming Programs FAQ

Learning to swim can reduce the risk of drowning by 88% and helps build confidence and water safety skills.
At Splashes Swim School, we provide a safe, fun, and engaging environment where children build confidence in the water. Our certified instructors, small class sizes, and flexible schedules ensure personalized learning at every level. With a focus on water safety and skill development, we make swimming both enjoyable and essential. Whether your child is a beginner or advanced swimmer, we help them thrive in a supportive setting.
It depends on the age, frequency, and experience level, but most children take 10-20 lessons to learn basic swimming skills.
