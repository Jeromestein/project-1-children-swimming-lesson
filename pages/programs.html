Our Swimming Programs

Toddler Swimming (Ages 1-3)

Introduction to water confidence and basic water safety skills through fun activities and parent participation.

  • Water acclimation activities to build confidence
  • Introduction to floating and kicking movements
  • Parent participation for a safe and fun experience
  • Songs and games to make learning engaging
  • Basic water safety and breath control

Preschool Swimming (Ages 3-5)

Basic swimming techniques and water safety skills in a playful, encouraging environment.

  • Fundamental swimming techniques like floating and kicking
  • Water play to encourage confidence in the pool
  • Learning to submerge and control breathing
  • Group activities to foster social interaction
  • Emphasis on safety rules and pool etiquette

Learn to Swim (Ages 6-12)

Comprehensive swimming instruction covering all major strokes and water safety skills.

  • Instruction on freestyle, backstroke, and breaststroke
  • Developing breath control and stroke coordination
  • Safe diving techniques and treading water
  • Endurance-building exercises
  • Water safety skills and self-rescue techniques

Advanced Swimming (Ages 8-12)

Perfect swimming techniques and build endurance for confident swimmers.

  • Refining freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly
  • Improving endurance for long-distance swimming
  • Learning advanced turns and competitive starts
  • Introduction to swim drills and interval training
  • Emphasizing strength, speed, and efficiency in the water
